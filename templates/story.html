Our Story

Yana’s Gallery began as a shared passion for creativity, art, and handmade craftsmanship. What started as small artistic experiments among friends slowly grew into a meaningful journey — a journey built on dedication, imagination, and the courage to turn passion into purpose.



At Yana’s Gallery, every creation is unique. From expressive canvas paintings to intricately designed Kundan and thread-work bangles, and hand-painted sarees that blend tradition with elegance — each piece is crafted carefully and exists as a single original creation.



We do not mass-produce.
We do not duplicate emotions.
Every product is made only once.



Because art is personal. Art carries emotion. And emotion cannot be copied.



Each creation tells its own story — made with patience, care, and intention. When you own a piece from Yana’s Gallery, you own something truly one of a kind.



This journey is not just about selling handmade creations. It is about celebrating originality, preserving emotion, and building something meaningful — together.



And this is only the beginning.
