Our Story

Where creativity meets emotion. Where art is not duplicated. Where every piece exists only once.

Yana’s Gallery began as a shared passion for creativity, art, and handmade craftsmanship. What started as small artistic experiments slowly grew into a meaningful journey — a journey built on dedication, imagination, and the courage to turn passion into purpose.

From expressive canvas paintings to intricately designed Kundan and thread-work bangles, and hand-painted sarees that blend tradition with elegance — each piece is crafted carefully and exists as a single original creation.

We do not mass-produce.

We do not duplicate emotions.

Every product is made only once.

Each creation tells its own story — made with patience, care, and intention. When you own a piece from Yana’s Gallery, you own something truly one of a kind.

And this is only the beginning.
